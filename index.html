Frequently Asked Questions

What is Gensyn?

Gensyn is a decentralized protocol that creates a global marketplace for machine learning computation. It connects anyone with spare computing power to developers who need it for AI training, making ML infrastructure accessible and affordable for everyone.

Do I need a powerful GPU to participate?

No! While GPUs provide better performance, you can participate with CPU-only systems (32GB RAM minimum), rent affordable cloud GPUs (from $0.30/hr), or use lower-end GPUs (8GB+ VRAM). Start with what you have!

How do I earn rewards?

By running RL Swarm nodes, you contribute compute to train AI models collaboratively. Participants earn on-chain rewards tracked on the Gensyn testnet. Your contributions are recorded and rewarded based on your computational contribution.

What is RL Swarm?

RL Swarm is a peer-to-peer system where multiple AI models train collaboratively, sharing insights and learning from collective experiences. It's faster and more efficient than training models in isolation.

Is Gensyn live on mainnet?

Gensyn is currently running on testnet with over 12,000 active nodes. The mainnet launch will come after thorough testing. You can participate in the testnet now to learn and prepare for mainnet rewards.

Where can I get help?

Join the Discord community for real-time support, check GitHub for technical issues, or use this website's comprehensive guides. The community is very helpful and active!
